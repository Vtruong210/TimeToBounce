Solution

The solution, an app that allows one to see what the usual trends are for basketball courts around the city. The app has set data of the tendencies from people who play there frequently at all hours of the data. An individual is able to just search up a day and time of the day and they will be able to see how crowded the basketball courts usually are during that time.

Time to Bounce is a simple and easy solution that does not quite have any other similar solutions. It is a local app that is very easy to use and access. The idea is only at its base level, but has much room to improve and grow.
